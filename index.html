About the Doctor

A physician who treats people, not just symptoms.

Dr. Md. Mashiour Rahman Rikabder (Rizvi) is an Associate Professor at Jahurul Islam Medical College & Hospital, where he blends clinical practice with academic rigor. With qualifications spanning general medicine, diabetes care, forensic medicine, and sexual medicine, his approach is grounded in evidence, humility, and respect for every patient who walks through his door.

His consultancy focuses on long-term management of diabetes and lifestyle-related conditions, careful diagnosis of complex general medicine cases, and preventive health strategies that help patients avoid complications before they begin. He believes a good physician explains — not prescribes in silence — and that patients who understand their condition heal better.

Beyond the chamber, Dr. Rizvi mentors medical students, contributes to clinical training at Jahurul Islam Medical College, and continues to expand his own expertise through international fellowships.
